About Us

The Technology Incubation and Entrepreneurship Development cell of JUIT aims at developing an entrepreneurial spirit and shares a vision to foster innovation and budding ideas amongst the students . The cell facilitates with a practical edge of exposing the students to hands-on experience of entrepreneurship through initiatives like Startup Simulator, E-summit, Networking Series, alumini interactions and interactive sessions with existing Entrepreneurs and Venture Capitalists. TIEDC is supported by the Department of Industries, Himachal Pradesh under Chief Minister’s Start-up/Innovation Projects/New Industries Scheme.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why to join TIEDC?

TIEDC will help you, improve your soft skills, induce innovation and entrepreneurship in you. Apart from technical skills, a person requires some soft skills and professionalism which by the help of TIEDC will be developed over time. Also, this will help you establish connections and your social presence.

Will TIEDC increase my job aspects?

TIEDC will help you develop several soft skills and professional skills. Combined with your technical skills, this will definitely give you an advantage over your competitors.

Is there any Membership Fee?

There’s no membership fee to join and become member of the cell.

How to become a member?
